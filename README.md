# 🌬️ Breathora: The "No-Friction" Reset Tool

**Breathora** is a high-performance breathing utility designed for acute stress, workplace resets, and high-pressure professionals. 

### Why Breathora?
* **Zero Friction:** No login, no ads, no subscriptions.
* **Vagus Nerve Focus:** Optimized 4-8 and Physiological Sigh patterns.
* **Built for the Road:** Audio-first "beep" cues designed for CarPlay and hands-free use.
* **PWA Ready:** Install it on your home screen directly from the browser.

### How to use:
1. Open the (https://patghmcc-rgb.github.io/stress-breathing/)
2. Select "Manager Reset" or "Panic Mode"
3. Close your eyes and follow the beeps.
